Engaging in the process of crafting a typeface has provided valuable insights into the intricacies of typeface creation. The research involved in understanding the grid system, along with a careful recreation, was instrumental in gaining a comprehensive understanding of its design principles.

Investing time in exploring the grid system not only clarified the technical aspects but also revealed the underlying rationale. Paper-based research played a pivotal role in crystallizing the vision and determining the desired elements for incorporation into the typeface.

The experience with professional software, particularly FontLab 7, significantly enhanced proficiency in type design. The font is now available for download, and updates are anticipated in the future. Stay informed for further developments in this ongoing project.
