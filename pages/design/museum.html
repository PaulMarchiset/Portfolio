For the branding class at Vanier College, we had the task of analyzing a brand, for me the Centre Georges Pompidou was my case study to understand its mission, vision, values, color schemes, and typography. This analysis laid the groundwork for my creation of the Centre Marchiset, a photography museum.

The practical implementation involved the design of a logo, an animated representation, posters, and a comprehensive brand guide. This guide meticulously outlines the utilization of various brand elements, such as typography, graphic elements, grid system, and provides detailed mockups for posters, websites, cards...

Utilizing tools like Figma, Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, and After Effects, the project facilitated a practical exploration of brand creation and the development of a brand identity within its contextual environment.
