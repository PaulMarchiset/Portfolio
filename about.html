Paul Marchiset, a videographer, and graphic designer is currently pursuing studies in multimedia and internet professions in Montbéliard. He stands out within team projects for his ability to take on responsibilities and steer operations effectively.

During his exchange at Vanier College in Montreal, Paul had the opportunity to deepen his passion for typography, focusing on the design and understanding of characters.

His strong interest in audiovisual content has led him to engage in various professional and academic projects, such as live broadcasts, documentaries, short films, and press conferences. This versatility reflects his willingness to explore different aspects of the field.

Beyond his accomplishments, Paul Marchiset remains aware of the constant need for learning. His academic and professional journey reflects his commitment to continuous improvement, always seeking to broaden his horizons and take on new challenges for personal and professional growth.
